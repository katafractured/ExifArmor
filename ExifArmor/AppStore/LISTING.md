## Description

Did you know every photo you share contains hidden metadata? Your exact GPS coordinates, device model, the time it was taken — all embedded invisibly in every image.

ExifArmor shows you exactly what your photos reveal. Then strips it with one tap.

HOW IT WORKS

Select any photo from your library. ExifArmor instantly scans for hidden metadata and displays everything that's exposed — including your location on a map. When you're ready, tap Strip and a clean copy is created. Your original is never touched.

WHAT METADATA IS HIDING IN YOUR PHOTOS

· GPS coordinates (your exact location, shown on a map)
· Device make and model (what phone you use)
· Date and time (exactly when you took the photo)
· Camera settings (lens, aperture, ISO, shutter speed)
· Software version (what OS you're running)
· Altitude (how high up you were)

WHY IT MATTERS

Every time you share a photo on a marketplace, dating app, or social media, you may be sharing your home address, workplace, or daily routine without knowing it.

ExifArmor is built for:
· Online marketplace sellers who don't want buyers knowing their home address
· Anyone sharing photos on dating apps
· Parents sharing photos of their children
· Privacy-conscious individuals
· Journalists and activists

FEATURES

· Exposure Preview — See exactly what your photos reveal, with GPS plotted on a map
· One-Tap Strip — Remove all metadata instantly
· Batch Mode — Clean larger batches of photos at once (Pro)

YOUR PRIVACY IS THE WHOLE POINT

ExifArmor contains zero analytics, zero trackers, and zero network calls. We don't collect data — we help you stop sharing it. Your photos never leave your device.
