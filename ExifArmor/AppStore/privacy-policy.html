Privacy Policy

ExifArmor by Katafract
Last updated: March 2026

ExifArmor is designed to protect your privacy, not compromise it. The app never connects to the internet, never transmits your photos, and collects zero personal data.

What Data Does ExifArmor Collect?

None. ExifArmor does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

Data Type Collected?
Photos or image content Never
Photo metadata (EXIF, GPS, etc.) Never
Device identifiers (IDFA, etc.) Never
Location data Never
Usage analytics Never*
Crash logs Never
Account or login information Never

*If you choose to opt in to anonymous analytics in Settings, ExifArmor will send anonymized, non-identifiable usage counts (such as "feature X was used 3 times") to help us improve the app. This contains no device IDs, no user IDs, no photos, and no personal information. This is off by default and entirely optional.

Photo Library Access

ExifArmor requests access to your photo library so it can read metadata from photos you select and save cleaned copies back to your library. Your photos are processed entirely on your device. No photo data is ever transmitted anywhere.

How Metadata Stripping Works

When you strip a photo, ExifArmor creates a new copy of the image with the selected metadata removed. The original photo in your library is never modified. The cleaned copy is saved to your photo library or shared via the system share sheet — both of which are handled by iOS, not by our servers.

In-App Purchases

ExifArmor Pro is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information, Apple ID, or any billing details. Purchase verification is handled on-device by Apple's StoreKit framework.

Share Extension

The ExifArmor share extension processes images locally on your device. When you use the share extension from another app, the image is stripped of metadata in memory and the cleaned version is returned. No data leaves your device during this process.

Third-Party Services

ExifArmor does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, social media SDKs, or data brokers. There are no third-party frameworks in the app that collect or transmit data.

Children's Privacy

ExifArmor does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

Data Retention

Since ExifArmor collects no data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Your privacy report statistics (number of photos stripped, badges earned) are stored locally on your device in standard iOS storage and are deleted if you uninstall the app.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new date. Since we don't collect email addresses or contact information, we cannot notify you directly — please check this page periodically.
